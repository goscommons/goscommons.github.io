How it works

We share validated designs that people can replicate to leverage sustainable impact.

We use and teach others how to use open collaborative practices and tools to facilitate decentralized collective design in different projects.

We also help makers, engineers and developers to grow the collaboration network.

We use together with our contributors and association collaborative economy business models to realize the vision of sustainable development and economic empowerment.

Helping others to raise funds, by preparing crowdfunding campaigns, and grant application is part of our core activities.

Where to start?
